# CBC Curriculum Map — Grades 1-3

## Grade 1
### English
- Alphabet: capitals, small letters, letter names, letter sounds
- Phonics: single consonants, short vowels, CVC words
- Sight words: 20 high-frequency words
- Listening & Speaking: greetings, introduce self
- Reading: picture-word matching, decode 2-3 word captions

### Kiswahili
- Herufi: alfabeti, sauti za herufi
- Maneno ya kawaida: salamu, vitu vya nyumbani
- Kusoma kwa picha: maneno mafupi, sentensi fupi

### Mathematics
- Numbers: 1-20 counting, number formation
- Addition/Subtraction: within 20
- Shapes & Patterns: circle, square, triangle
- Measurement: compare lengths
